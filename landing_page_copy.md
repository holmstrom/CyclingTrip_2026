# ALPS 2026: The Expedition
## 8 Riders. 3 Legends. 1 Unforgettable Week.

---

### The Mission
**June 3 - June 6, 2026**

We are heading to the holy grail of cycling. Le Bourg-d'Oisans isn't just a village; it's the stadium where cycling history is written. For 4 days, we will call this valley home, conquer the giants of the Tour de France, and celebrate with the finest food and wine the region has to offer.

---

### 1. The Rides: The Trilogy
*Three days. Three iconic challenges. Full routes linked below.*

#### **Day 1: The Legend (Alpe d'Huez Loop)**
The warm-up. We tackle the 21 hairpins immediately.
*   **Route**: Bourg d'Oisans -> Alpe d'Huez -> Col de Sarenne -> Mizoën -> Bourg.
*   **Stats**: 55km | 1,600m Elevation
*   **Link**: [View Full Route on Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/tour/search?q=Alpe+d%27Huez+Sarenne)
*   **Highlight**: Coffee at the top, then the wild descent via Sarenne where the pros rarely go.

#### **Day 2: The Beauty (Croix de Fer Loop)**
Often voted the most beautiful climb in the Alps.
*   **Route**: Bourg -> Allemond -> Col du Glandon -> Col de la Croix de Fer -> Saint-Sorlin -> Bourg.
*   **Stats**: 65km | 1,700m Elevation
*   **Link**: [View Full Route on Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/tour/search?q=Croix+de+Fer+Loop)
*   **Highlight**: Riding past the turquoise Lac de Grand Maison.

#### **Day 3: The Giant (Galibier)**
The roof of the Tour. We touch the sky at 2,642m.
*   **Route**: Van transfer to Lautaret -> Galibier Summit -> Descent to Bourg (Optional full ride for the brave).
*   **Stats**: 40km - 90km | 1,200m+ Elevation
*   **Link**: [View Full Route on Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/tour/search?q=Galibier+from+Lautaret)
*   **Highlight**: The final kilometer where the air gets thin and the views stretch to Italy.

### 2. Accommodation
*Choose your level of comfort. All options are cyclist-friendly.*

#### **Option A: High-End (The Luxury Chalet)**
*   **Name**: **Chalet Ilfer / Chalet Ribot**
*   **Location**: Vaujany / Outskirts.
*   **Vibe**: Private sanctuary. Sauna, huge kitchen, private terrace. The full pro-team experience.
*   **Price**: ~€125 pp/night.

#### **Option B: Medium (The Cyclist's Hub)**
*   **Name**: **Hotel de Milan**
*   **Location**: Downtown Bourg d'Oisans.
*   **Vibe**: The heart of the action. Historic cycling hotel, buzzing with riders.
*   **Price**: ~€75 pp/night.

#### **Option C: Economy (The Adventurer)**
*   **Name**: **Camping La Cascade (Cottages)** or **Hotel Le Dome**
*   **Location**: Bourg d'Oisans / Alpe d'Huez.
*   **Vibe**: Simple, clean, effective. Save money for better wine.
*   **Price**: ~€40-50 pp/night.

### 3. Gastronomy
*Fueling the machine.*

#### **Option A: High-End (The Michelin Star)**
*   **Name**: **Le P'tit Polyte**
*   **Style**: 1 Michelin Star. Exquisite alpine tasting menu.
*   **Cost**: ~€130 pp.

#### **Option B: Medium (The Brasserie)**
*   **Name**: **La Romanche**
*   **Style**: Quality steaks, pasta, and local specialties. Lively atmosphere.
*   **Cost**: ~€45 pp.

#### **Option C: Economy (Casual Refuel)**
*   **Name**: **Le Tremplin** / **Smithy's Tavern**
*   **Style**: Best pizzas in town, burgers, hearty portions.
*   **Cost**: ~€20-25 pp.
